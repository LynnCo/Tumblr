If not, choose from hundreds of beautiful themes in the <a href="/themes">Theme Garden</a>.</p><h2><a id="introduction" style="margin-top:40px;">Introduction</a></h2><p>Tumblr is built around seven key post types: text, photos, links, quotes, chat, audio, and video. Be sure to customize your theme around these types so that your posts show up beautifully on your blog, on the Dashboards of your followers, and on other blogs when they&rsquo;re reblogged.</p><a class="custom_themes__ga_dash_banner" target="_blank" href="https://dash.generalassemb.ly/tumblr" title="Take class in theme design with GA Dash"><span class="custom_themes__ga_dash_banner__button">Learn more</span></a><h2><a id="pro_tips" style="margin-top:40px;">Pro-tips</a></h2><p>Try to avoid posting external images and embedded media in text posts. It&rsquo;s less engaging on the Dashboard and can be problematic with custom themes. Instead, create a video post for a video, a photo post for photos, and so on and so forth.</p><h2><a id="get_started" style="margin-top:40px;">Get Started</a></h2><ol><li>Click the name of your blog at the top of the Dashboard or under the list icon at the top.</li><li>Click &ldquo;Customize&rdquo; on the right column.</li><li>Click &ldquo;Edit HTML&rdquo; below the theme thumbnail on the left.</li></ol><p>Tumblr has two types of special operators used to render content in your HTML.</p><p><b>Variables</b> are used to insert dynamic data like your blog's title or description:</p><h4 style="margin-bottom:-5px;">Example</h4><pre>
&lt;html>
    &lt;head>
        &lt;title><b class="variable">{Title}</b>&lt;/title>
    &lt;/head>
    &lt;body>
        ...
    &lt;/body>

&lt;html>
